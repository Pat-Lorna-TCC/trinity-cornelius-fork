## Core Framework

The defining pattern of 2024-2025 longevity research is the convergence of radically different interventions on three upstream mechanisms of cognitive aging: **(1) metabolic dysfunction, (2) neuroinflammation, and (3) circadian dysregulation.** This explains why interventions as diverse as a diabetes drug (metformin), a fasting window, an obesity drug (semaglutide), exercise, and a soil bacterium (gut microbiome) all protect cognition - they're all addressing the same root causes.

Brain aging is not primarily neuronal death but **metabolic failure, circadian dysregulation, neuroinflammation, and waste accumulation** - all substantially modifiable.

---

## The Three Upstream Mechanisms

### Mechanism 1: Metabolic Dysfunction
- **What it is**: Insulin resistance, altered glucose metabolism, mitochondrial inefficiency
- **How it drives cognitive aging**: Brain cells starved of glucose → energy failure → synaptic dysfunction → neurodegeneration
- **Interventions that target it**: Ketogenic diets (alternative fuel via ketones), TRE (circadian metabolic alignment), metformin (AMPK/energy sensing), GLP-1 agonists (glucose regulation), NAD+ precursors (mitochondrial cofactor), exercise (lactate + metabolic stress)

### Mechanism 2: Neuroinflammation
- **What it is**: Chronic glial activation, NLRP3 inflammasome activity, inflammatory cytokines (IL-1beta, IL-6, TNF-alpha)
- **How it drives cognitive aging**: Inflammatory microglia damage synapses → tau phosphorylation accelerates → self-amplifying tau-NLRP3 loop
- **Interventions that target it**: Exercise (anti-inflammatory myokines), sleep/glymphatic clearance, TRE (hippocampal inflammation gene normalization), metformin (NF-kappaB), gut microbiome (SCFA, immune regulation), GLP-1 agonists (NLRP3 direct suppression), senolytics (eliminate SASP-secreting cells)

### Mechanism 3: Circadian Dysregulation
- **What it is**: Disrupted clock gene expression, misaligned eating/sleep timing, weakened circadian activity rhythms
- **How it drives cognitive aging**: Disrupted circadian rhythms suppress glymphatic clearance, impair hippocampal gene repair, accelerate AD pathology
- **Interventions that target it**: TRE (primary peripheral clock synchronizer), consistent sleep schedule, morning light exposure, early eating window

## The Personalized Optimization Implication

This convergence framework enables personalized stacking logic:
- **Metabolic priority**: If CGM shows insulin resistance → prioritize TRE (early window), resistance training, consider metformin
- **Inflammation priority**: If GFAP or CRP elevated → prioritize gut microbiome optimization, quercetin + fisetin protocol, omega-3s
- **Circadian priority**: If sleep architecture disrupted, late eating pattern → prioritize TRE alignment, consistent sleep schedule, morning light

The biomarker infrastructure now exists to identify which upstream mechanism is most dysregulated: p-tau217 (AD pathology), GFAP (neuroinflammation), DunedinPACE (biological aging pace), HRV (autonomic/circadian health), sleep architecture tracking.
