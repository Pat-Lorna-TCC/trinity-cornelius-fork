## Core Framework

Flow phenomenology emerges from **computational optimization** within active inference framework:

1. **High precision weighting** on:
   - Expected sensory consequences of action
   - Sequential action unfolding
2. **Suppressed counterfactual planning** - attention wholly focused on immediate task demands
3. **Inhibited deep deliberation** - no "stepping back" to consider alternatives

**Key Insight**: Flow achieves "the elimination of reflective self-awareness" while maintaining skilled agency by deploying attention exclusively to rapid task-specific demands that **only expertise can track**.

---

## Mechanism - How Flow Works Computationally

### Attention Deployment as Core Mechanism

Flow emerges from **strategic attention allocation**:
- **High precision weighting** on immediate sensory-motor predictions
- **Suppression** of counterfactual planning and self-referential thought
- **Exclusive focus** on rapidly changing task demands

### Why Expertise is Required

**Mechanistic Necessity**: Only experts can track rapid task fluctuations without deliberation.

**Computational Logic**:
- Novices require System 2 deliberation to perform
- Experts have System 1 automaticity
- Flow requires sufficient automaticity to operate without meta-cognitive monitoring

**Result**: The expertise prerequisite is not arbitrary - it's mechanistically necessary for the computational optimization to work.

This computational model explains multiple flow characteristics:
