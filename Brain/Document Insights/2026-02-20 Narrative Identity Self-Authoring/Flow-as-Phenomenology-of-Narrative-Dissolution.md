## The Synthesis

Existing knowledge in the vault establishes:
- Flow is a selfless state - flow involves DMN deactivation
- Default Mode Network (DMN) - DMN is the narrative self-construction engine
- New 2025 research establishes: DMN performs "situation model maintenance" - specifically maintaining the coherent narrative context of "who I am in this ongoing life story"

**The Novel Connection**:
Flow does not merely suppress self-referential thinking (as previously framed) - it **dissolves the situation model itself**. During peak flow, the practitioner loses their sense of being "a person in an ongoing life story" - not just their internal monologue, but the narrative frame within which events acquire personal meaning.

This reframes flow from "absence of self-talk" to "temporary narrative dissolution" - a phenomenological state that Buddhist practice describes through different language (anatta, no-self) and that narrative psychology is now equipped to describe mechanically.

## The Hypothesis

**If flow = narrative dissolution (not just self-talk suppression), then:**

1. Flow states provide the experiential reference point for "what it's like not to be captured by your story" - the phenomenological knowledge that identity is constructed, not essential

2. This experiential knowledge is the substrate for **identity flexibility** - people who regularly access flow states may develop better narrative flexibility not just through cognitive insight but through embodied experiential memory of narrative absence

3. Buddhist practice and flow training achieve the same experiential outcome through different mechanisms:
   - Meditation: gradual DMN quieting through attention training
   - Flow: sudden DMN suppression through challenge-skill balance
   - Both: provide repeated experiential access to "self without narrative"
