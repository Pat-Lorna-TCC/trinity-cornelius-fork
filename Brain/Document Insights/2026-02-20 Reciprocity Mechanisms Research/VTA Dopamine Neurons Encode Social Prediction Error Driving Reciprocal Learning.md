## Core Insight

VTA dopamine neurons encode **social prediction error** - the difference between expected and actual social outcomes - and this activity drives social reinforcement learning and motivated reciprocal behavior. Social reciprocity operates through the **same reward prediction error mechanisms** as non-social rewards.

---

## Evidence

**Study Design**: Neuroscience research on VTA dopamine neuron activity

**Key Results**:
- VTA dopamine neurons increase activity during interactions with unfamiliar conspecifics
- Neurons display heterogeneous responses to social encounters
- Activity encodes social prediction error (difference between expected and actual social outcomes)
- This drives social reinforcement learning and motivated social behavior
- Chemogenetic inhibition of VTA dopamine neurons **attenuates exploration** toward unfamiliar conspecifics
