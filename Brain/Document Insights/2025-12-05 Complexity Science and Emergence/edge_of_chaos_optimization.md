## Core Framework

Complex adaptive systems across domains optimize performance by operating at the boundary between order and chaos - the "edge of chaos." This critical point enables:
- Maximum computational power
- Optimal information processing
- Adaptive flexibility
- Evolvability and learning

## Why Edge of Chaos is Optimal

**Order Region (Frozen)**:
- Predictable, stable
- Low computational power
- Cannot adapt or learn
- Gets stuck in local optima

**Chaos Region (Random)**:
- High variability
- No stable information processing
- Cannot maintain coherent patterns
- Pure noise, no signal

**Edge of Chaos (Critical)**:
- Maximum information storage AND transmission
- Stable enough to maintain patterns
- Flexible enough to adapt
- Power-law avalanches (scale-free dynamics)
- Optimal for computation and control
