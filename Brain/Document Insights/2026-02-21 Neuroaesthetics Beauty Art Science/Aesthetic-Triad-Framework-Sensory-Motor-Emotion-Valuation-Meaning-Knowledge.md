## Core Framework

The Aesthetic Triad is the most influential theoretical model in contemporary neuroaesthetics. It proposes that aesthetic experience is generated by three interacting systems:

```
AESTHETIC TRIAD
    |
    |--- SENSORY-MOTOR SYSTEM
    |    (perception, embodied simulation, motor resonance)
    |    Key regions: visual/auditory cortex, motor cortex, mirror neurons
    |
    |--- EMOTION-VALUATION SYSTEM
    |    (reward, dopamine, hedonic tone)
    |    Key regions: striatum, nucleus accumbens, mOFC, amygdala
    |
    |--- MEANING-KNOWLEDGE SYSTEM
         (context, culture, memory, intentionality)
         Key regions: DMN, vmPFC, inferior frontal gyrus
```

## Context & Acceptance

**Field Acceptance**: Widely accepted as organizing framework; used across clinical, experimental, and therapeutic neuroaesthetics
**Key Applications in 2024-2025**:
- Extended to explain neurodegenerative disease effects (each disease targets different triad components)
- Applied to psychedelic aesthetics (psychedelics modulate all three simultaneously)
- Used to create the Psychedelic Aesthetic Experience Questionnaire (PAEQ)
- Applied to explain why AI art triggers different responses (lacks perceived meaning-knowledge intentionality)
- Confirmed as domain-specific (visual art vs. music weight the three components differently)

## 2024-2025 Elaborations

### Domain-Specific Weighting
- Visual art experience: Meaning-knowledge system dominant (vmPFC, IFG, DMN) - introspective mode
- Music experience: Emotion-valuation system dominant (striatum, STG) - reward/immersion mode
- Mathematical beauty: Meaning-knowledge system dominant - requires expertise to generate sensory-motor/emotional components

### Disease Dissociation (Clinical Test of the Model)
| Disease | Primary System Affected | Paradoxical Effect |
|---------|------------------------|-------------------|
| Alzheimer's | Meaning-knowledge (medial temporal) | Emotion-valuation (music response) preserved |
| Parkinson's | Emotion-valuation (dopaminergic) | Artistic emergence with medication changes |
| FTD | Meaning-knowledge (inhibitory circuits) | Sensory-motor disinhibition creates art output |
| Huntington's | Emotion-valuation (striatal) | Sensory-motor preserved; no emotional resonance |

### AI Art Bias Through the Triad
- Human art: All three systems engage (sensory input + emotional reward + intentionality attribution)
- AI art (known): Meaning-knowledge system fails to activate intentionality; emotional response reduced
- AI art (unknown): All three systems engage normally

## Why the Triad Matters for Understanding Consciousness

The Aesthetic Triad provides a three-system account of a peculiarly rich form of consciousness: the aesthetic experience. By identifying three separable and dissociable components, it allows:
- Targeting specific components therapeutically
- Understanding why different people find different things beautiful (different system weights)
- Explaining how the same work can be beautiful to one person and cold to another (different meaning-knowledge activation based on cultural training)
