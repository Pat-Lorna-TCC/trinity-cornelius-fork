## Core Insight

EFTeacher is the first systematic attempt to automate Episodic Future Thinking delivery at scale using a large language model (GPT-4-Turbo). By generating personalized positive future cues tailored to individual goals and regulatory focus, it makes imagination-based behavioral interventions accessible without human therapists. This represents a new category: AI-augmented imagination interventions.

---

## Evidence

**System design**: AI chatbot powered by GPT-4-Turbo generates personalized EFT cues
**Key design principle**: Personalized positive future cues expand the "temporal window" - the ability to think further into the future when evaluating present choices
**Significance**: First scalable LLM-delivered imagination-based intervention
**Status**: arXiv preprint (2025) - early-stage, not yet validated in large RCT

**EFT mechanism**: Imagining specific, vivid personal future scenarios before making choices reduces temporal discounting - the systematic over-valuation of immediate rewards over future ones (meta-analysis: g=0.52 effect size)

---

## Why Personalization Matters for EFT

Standard EFT uses generic future cues (think of any positive future event). EFTeacher's personalization matters because:
1. Individual differences in regulatory focus (promotion vs. prevention) moderate EFT effects
2. Goal-relevant future cues create stronger temporal window expansion than generic cues
3. LLM personalization can tailor cues to individual goals, values, and life contexts at scale

**The scaling breakthrough**: EFT delivery previously required trained human facilitators or standardized laboratory protocols. EFTeacher demonstrates that LLMs can deliver the personalization component at zero marginal cost per user.
