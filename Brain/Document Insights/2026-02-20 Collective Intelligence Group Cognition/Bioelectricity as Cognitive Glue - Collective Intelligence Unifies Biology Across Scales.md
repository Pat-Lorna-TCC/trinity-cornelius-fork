## Core Framework

Michael Levin's lab proposes that **bioelectricity serves as "cognitive glue"** - the mechanism by which cellular collectives achieve coordinated intelligence without neural networks. Non-neural bioelectrical networks throughout the body process information in ways that enable cells to achieve morphological goals through distributed electrical signaling. This reframes biology: collective intelligence is not an emergent property of neurons alone but a **fundamental organizing principle at every biological scale**.

---

## Two Key Demonstrations

### 1. Planaria Head Regeneration
Bioelectric differentials determine the number and location of heads during regeneration in planaria (flatworms). The collective of cells "knows" the target morphology through distributed electrical signaling - not through genetic program execution. The morphological goal is encoded in the bioelectric field, not in individual cell genetics.

**Implication**: Body shape is a collective intelligence achievement, not just a genetic blueprint.

### 2. Cancer as Collective Intelligence Failure
Cancer can be **induced without DNA damage** by targeting the bioelectric state of "instructor cells." Cancer is reframed not as a genetic mutation problem but as a **failure of collective intelligence** - cells lose access to the tissue's information structure (the bioelectric field) and revert to ancient unicellular behavioral programs.

**Implication**: Cancer is what individual cells do when they lose access to the collective. This reframes treatment: instead of attacking individual cancer cells, interventions could restore collective bioelectric coherence.

## The Deeper Principle

Levin's framework suggests that **intelligence is substrate-independent** not just at the abstract level (neurons, humans, AI agents) but at the molecular-to-organism level within biology itself. The same principle - distributed information processing enabling goal-directed collective behavior - operates at:
- Molecular signaling networks
- Cellular collectives
- Organ systems
- Organisms

This means the intelligence of a human body is not just "in the brain" but distributed across all bioelectric networks throughout the body. **Interoception** (sensing internal body states) is not just metabolic monitoring - it is partial access to the distributed intelligence of the cellular collective.
