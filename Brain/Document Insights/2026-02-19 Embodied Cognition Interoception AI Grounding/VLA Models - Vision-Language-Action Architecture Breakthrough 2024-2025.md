## Core Insight

Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models represent the most significant architectural advance in embodied AI during 2024-2025, demonstrating unprecedented generalization across tasks and robot platforms by directly mapping visual observations and language instructions to motor actions without explicit symbolic grounding. Models like **OpenVLA**, **π₀**, **RT-2**, and **Gemini Robotics** bypass the symbol grounding problem through end-to-end learned sensorimotor representations.

---

## Evidence

**Major VLA Models (2024-2025)**:
- **OpenVLA** - Open-source VLA with cross-embodiment transfer
- **π₀ (Pi-Zero)** - Zero-shot policy transfer across robot morphologies
- **RT-2 (Robotic Transformer 2)** - Google's vision-language-action model
- **Gemini Robotics** - Multimodal foundation model for robotics

**Key Capabilities**:
1. **Direct perception-to-action mapping** (no symbolic intermediate)
2. **Cross-embodiment generalization** (works on different robot bodies)
3. **Language-conditioned control** (natural language task specification)
4. **Few-shot task adaptation** (minimal examples for new tasks)
5. **Zero-shot transfer** (performs novel tasks without fine-tuning)

**Conference Coverage**:
- NeurIPS 2024-2025: Multiple workshops on embodied AI
- ICLR 2024-2025: VLA architecture sessions
- RSS 2025: Robotics + large models integration
- 47 VLA models documented in research period (2022-2025)

**Why This Is Transformative**:
Traditional robotics: Perception → Symbolic planning → Control
VLA models: Visual input + Language → Action (end-to-end)

This sidesteps decades of symbolic AI challenges by learning grounding through massive sensorimotor data rather than hand-coding it.
