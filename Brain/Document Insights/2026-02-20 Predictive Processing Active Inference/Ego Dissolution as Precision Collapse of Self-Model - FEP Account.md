## Core Framework

The Free Energy Principle provides a formal computational account of ego dissolution: the self is not a fixed entity but a **high-precision prediction** - a set of prior beliefs about what kind of thing "I" am, with high confidence weights attached. Ego dissolution occurs when the **precision of the self-model collapses**.

**The mechanism:**
- Alpha waves (8-12 Hz) bind the sense of "I am" across brain regions - they are the precision-weighting signals that maintain the self-model
- During ego dissolution (measured with DMT and advanced meditation), alpha wave activity collapses
- Stronger alpha suppression = more intense ego dissolution
- **Interpretation**: Alpha rhythms implement precision weighting of the self-model. Ego = high-precision self-prediction. Ego dissolution = precision collapse of self-model.

## The Formal Computational Account

This gives a mathematical treatment of what Buddhism has described experientially for 2,500 years:

| Buddhist Concept | FEP Formalization |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| Anatta (no-self) | Self-model is a high-precision prediction, not a fixed reality |
| Ego dissolution | Precision collapse of self-model predictions |
| Meditation | Systematic precision attenuation training for the self-model |
| Cessation events | Generative model temporarily suspended, followed by reset to high-precision present-moment prediction |

The 2026 body-scan meditation paper adds: body-scan meditation leads to precision attenuation of the body's self-model boundaries, facilitating broader changes in self-concept. Meditators learn to view self-related processes as impermanent, flexible, dynamic - which is exactly what FEP predicts: reducing precision on self-model predictions makes those predictions more susceptible to updating.
