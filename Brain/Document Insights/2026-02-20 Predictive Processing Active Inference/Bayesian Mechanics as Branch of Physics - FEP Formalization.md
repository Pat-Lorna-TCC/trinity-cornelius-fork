## Core Framework

The 2024-2025 period has seen aggressive formalization of FEP as a branch of **physics** in its own right - not merely a neuroscience metaphor, but a fundamental mathematical principle occupying a position analogous to classical and quantum mechanics.

**The Core Claim:**
For any system with a **Markov blanket** (a statistical boundary separating internal from external states), there is a natural Bayesian mechanics that describes its dynamics as inference. This is not a biological claim about brains - it is a claim about the **mathematical structure of self-organizing systems generally**.

**The Path Integral Connection:**
- FEP connects to the principle of least action: internal paths of particles with conservative dynamics are always the most likely paths - paths of least action
- Action = path integral of surprisal (self-information)
- This places FEP in direct dialogue with quantum field theory and statistical mechanics

## The Markov Blanket as Universal Organizational Principle

A Markov blanket statistically separates "inside" from "outside":
- Neurons have Markov blankets (cell membrane)
- Organisms have Markov blankets (skin/sensory surface)
- Social groups have Markov blankets (cultural boundary)
- AI agents have Markov blankets (context window + action space)

Each scale exhibits Bayesian mechanics - the same mathematical principles apply from cells to civilizations.

**For AI agent architecture**: The Folder Paradigm describes agent directories as cognitive architecture. Bayesian mechanics formalizes this: the folder/context window IS the Markov blanket that creates the statistical boundary defining the agent.

## Critical Evaluation (Non-Falsifiability)

**This is the most contested claim in the FEP literature.** The 2025 "myth of the Bayesian brain" paper raises legitimate concerns:

**Friston's position**: FEP is a mathematical principle like calculus. You cannot falsify calculus. Only specific generative models instantiated within FEP are empirically testable.

**Critics' response**: This non-falsifiability is a bug, not a feature. FEP can explain any result post-hoc = scientifically dangerous flexibility.

**Additional formal critiques (Academia.edu, 2025)**:
1. Conflation of thermodynamic and information-theoretical "free energy" creates category errors
2. Causal relationships asserted across levels of description are explanatorily circular
3. Markov blanket formalism has formal ambiguities limiting universality claims

## Context & Acceptance

**Field Acceptance**: Controversial - genuinely divisive in philosophy of science and theoretical neuroscience

**The Literalist Fallacy (2025, British Journal for Philosophy of Science)**: Even if FEP is formally valid as physics, organisms need not literally compute FEP - they might approximate it through simpler mechanisms. Scientific realism does not require literal identity between model and modeled system.

**Honest Assessment**: This is intellectually exciting but epistemically risky. The formalization is elegant; the empirical grounding for the universality claims remains weak.
