# Interoception as Predictive Self-Model - EPIC Framework

**Source**: Embodied Predictive Interoception Coding (EPIC) model, 2024-2025; Greenwood & Garfinkel, Annual Review of Psychology (2025); Gabhart, Xiong & Bastos, Trends in Cognitive Sciences (2025); Silvanto, Frontiers in Psychology (2025)

## Core Finding

The 2024-2025 EPIC (Embodied Predictive Interoception Coding) model confirms and extends the predictive processing account of interoception:

- **Agranular visceromotor cortices** issue interoceptive predictions (top-down)
- **Interoceptive experience** largely reflects limbic predictions about expected body state, constrained (not determined) by ascending visceral sensations
- **Emotion** = top-down inference on multimodal causes of interoceptive afferents
- **Embodied selfhood** = "instrumental interoceptive inference" for allostatic regulation

**Key insight**: Emotions are not readouts of body states - they are predictions about body states. The brain predicts what the body should feel, and this prediction IS the emotion. The actual body signals merely constrain the prediction's precision.

---

## Emotion as Top-Down Prediction

This is a counter-intuitive reversal of folk psychology:

**Folk psychology**: I feel fear → therefore I perceive threat → therefore I act
**EPIC framework**: I predict threat → brain generates prediction of what fear should feel like in the body → if prediction matches ascending visceral signals → emotion is experienced

**Practical implication**: You can change emotional experience by changing the predictions, not just by changing the body state. This is the computational basis for:
- Cognitive reappraisal (changing the predictive interpretation)
- Meditation (reducing precision on interoceptive predictions)
- Exposure therapy (updating threat predictions through repeated disconfirmation)

## Aphantasia Finding (2025)

A particularly striking result: Reduced interoceptive precision disrupts the brain's ability to generate high-gain top-down predictions needed for **mental imagery** - not just emotion regulation.

People with aphantasia (inability to form mental images) show reduced interoceptive precision. This demonstrates that interoception is not just about body regulation - it provides the **precision infrastructure** for generating high-resolution top-down predictions across cognitive domains including imagination.

**Implication**: The body is not just along for the ride in cognition - interoceptive precision actively enables or limits cognitive operations including memory recall, imagination, and planning.

---

## Allostasis as Core Function

"Embodied selfhood as instrumental interoceptive inference for allostatic regulation" means:
- The self is not primarily a cognitive construct (a story the brain tells)
- The self is primarily an **interoceptive inference system** - a mechanism for predicting and regulating body states
- Allostasis (maintaining body within viable ranges) is the self's core computational function
- Everything else (identity, narrative self, social self) is built on top of this interoceptive foundation
