# Social Curiosity - The Mentalizing Network and Wanting to Know Other Minds

**Source**: Meta-analytic investigation of 108 social interaction neuroimaging studies, *Aperture Neuro*, 2025. doi:10.52294/001c.138339.
**Document Type**: Meta-Analysis (N=108 studies)

## Core Insight

Social curiosity - the drive to understand other minds, to know what others think, feel, and intend - operates through a distinct neural network (the mentalizing network) that is different from both epistemic curiosity (dopaminergic, knowledge gaps) and perceptual curiosity (vmPFC, sensory uncertainty). Together, these three systems represent a complete taxonomy of curiosity: about facts (epistemic), about sensory experience (perceptual), and about other minds (social).

---

## Evidence

**Study Design**: Meta-analysis of 108 social interaction neuroimaging studies; published 2025 in *Aperture Neuro*

**Core Social Curiosity Network**:

| Region | Function in Social Curiosity |
|--------|------------------------------|
| Bilateral TPJ (temporoparietal junction) | Belief attribution, theory of mind, social information sharing |
| dMPFC (dorsomedial prefrontal cortex) | Mental state reasoning ("what is she thinking?") |
| PCC/Precuneus | Episodic memory integration (connecting past experiences to understanding current social state) |
| Cerebellum Crus1/2 | Timing and social coordination support |

**Social information sharing and TPJ**:
- Reposting of negative social content correlates with TPJ-insula and TPJ-dlPFC connectivity
- TPJ calculates "social information value" - how worth-sharing is this information?
- This makes TPJ the neural substrate for the social spread of information (gossip, virality, etc.)

---

## Three-Type Curiosity Taxonomy (Synthesized)

| Type | Trigger | Key Brain Region | Primary System |
|------|---------|-----------------|----------------|
| Epistemic curiosity | Knowledge gap (prior knowledge + missing piece) | SN/VTA, hippocampus, caudate | Dopaminergic (System 1) |
| Perceptual curiosity | Sensory ambiguity (unclear what you're seeing/hearing) | OTC → vmPFC | LC-NE + vmPFC (System 2 + bridge) |
| Social curiosity | Want to know what others think/feel/intend | TPJ, dMPFC, PCC | Mentalizing network (System 3?) |

**All three converge on**: reducing prediction error about the world (informational, sensory, social). Social curiosity = minimizing free energy about other minds' generative models.

---

## Why Social Curiosity is Evolutionarily Primary

Social curiosity is likely the OLDEST form of curiosity from an evolutionary standpoint:
- Survival in social mammals required continuous monitoring of other group members' mental states (intentions, alliances, emotions)
- TPJ and mentalizing network are among the most evolutionarily recent cortical developments - but they build on older threat-detection and social bonding circuits
- "Gossip" is social curiosity expressed as information sharing: high social information value → TPJ activation → sharing

**Social media exploitation of social curiosity**:
- Social media platforms are optimized to continuously generate social information gaps ("what is X thinking about Y?")
- TPJ is maximally activated by social information, especially negative (threat-relevant social information)
- This is why social media is more addictive than other content: it simultaneously activates epistemic curiosity AND social curiosity
