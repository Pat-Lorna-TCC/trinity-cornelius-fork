## Core Framework

**Psychological safety is not a soft cultural value - it is the neurophysiological prerequisite for higher cognitive function.** The Polyvagal Theory framing: Ventral vagal complex (VVC) activation is the autonomic state from which genuine learning, creative thinking, social intelligence, and working memory capacity all emerge. Sympathetic activation or dorsal vagal shutdown redirect cognitive resources toward survival - making learning, creativity, and prosocial behavior neurologically unavailable.

---

## The Autonomic State - Cognitive Function Map

| Autonomic State | Neuroception | Cognitive Access |
|----------------|--------------|------------------|
| Ventral Vagal (VVC) active | SAFE | Full: learning, creativity, social engagement, empathy, working memory, executive function |
| Sympathetic activation | DANGER | Narrow: threat-relevant processing only, reduced creativity, impaired working memory |
| Dorsal Vagal (DVC) dominant | LIFE-THREAT | Minimal: conservation, reduced engagement, dissociation |

**Key insight**: This isn't a metaphor - it's an autonomic state constraint. Asking people to learn or create while in sympathetically activated states (threat, evaluation anxiety, social risk) is asking the nervous system to perform functions it has down-regulated as a survival optimization.

## Why "Psychological Safety Enables Velocity" is Deeper Than It Sounds

This connects to Psychological safety enables velocity not comfort but goes further:

The vault's existing insight frames psychological safety as a cognitive overhead reducer. The PVT framing adds a mechanistic dimension:
- **Sympathetic activation physically reduces working memory capacity** (HRV-cognition research shows this directly)
- **Social threat activates the same neurophysiological pathways as physical threat** - evaluative anxiety is not "just worry," it is an autonomic state shift
- **Co-regulation** (being with a calm, safe other) is a direct mechanism for shifting autonomic state - which is why therapeutic relationships and leadership presence have measurable cognitive effects

## Practical Implications

**For organizations and teams:**
- "Psychological safety" programs that focus only on culture miss the neurophysiological layer
- Leaders' autonomic state is transmitted to teams through vocal prosody, facial expression, and pacing (co-regulation)
- Meeting environments with time pressure, evaluation threat, and hierarchy cues activate sympathetic states that directly reduce the cognitive performance they're designed to elicit

**For education:**
- Ventral vagal activation is the neurophysiological prerequisite for genuine learning - not comfort or low standards
- Students in threat-activated states (test anxiety, social evaluation) have reduced cognitive resources available for learning, not just for performance
- Trauma-exposed and neurodivergent students may have chronically shifted neuroceptive thresholds - requiring environmental modifications, not just cognitive accommodations

**For therapy:**
- The therapeutic relationship works partly because the therapist's calm presence induces VVC activation via co-regulation
- Creative arts therapies operate through the same mechanism: safe creative engagement shifts autonomic state, which enables processing that purely cognitive approaches can't access
