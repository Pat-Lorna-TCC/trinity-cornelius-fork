## Synthesis

The 2025 *Nature Neuroscience* discovery that the DMN's insulated core protects internally-generated cognition from environmental disruption - and that this core is the primary meaning-construction machinery - creates a significant reinterpretation of flow states:

**When flow suppresses DMN activity, it suspends the meaning-construction apparatus itself.**

This means flow is not merely "selfless" in the Buddhist sense (no sense of separate self) - it is literally operating **below the level of meaning**. The moment-to-moment experience of flow is meaning-free, which is precisely why it feels both profound and ineffable: the machinery that would construct "this is meaningful" has been suspended.

**The Paradox**: People retrospectively report flow as among the most meaningful experiences of their lives - yet during flow, the meaning-construction system is suppressed.

**Resolution**: Meaning is constructed *about* flow in the reflective aftermath (DMN reactivates, integrates the experience into narrative), not *during* flow. The felt sense of meaning in flow is retrospective, not contemporaneous.
