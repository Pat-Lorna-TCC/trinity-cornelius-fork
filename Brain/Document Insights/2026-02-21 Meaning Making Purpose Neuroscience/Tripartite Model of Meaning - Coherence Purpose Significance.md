# Tripartite Model of Meaning - Coherence Purpose Significance

**Source**: Martela & Steger (2016, updated 2024-2025). "The influence mechanism underlying meaning in life." *Frontiers in Psychology*. https://public-pages-files-2025.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2024.1292996/pdf

## Core Framework

The dominant scientific framework for meaning in life comprises three distinguishable but highly correlated dimensions:

1. **Coherence/Comprehension**: Sense that life makes sense and is understandable - reality is predictable and interpretable
2. **Purpose**: Experience of being directed toward valued goals - having direction and motivation
3. **Significance/Mattering**: Sense of life's inherent value - that one's existence matters

---

## Context & Acceptance

**Field Acceptance**: Broadly accepted as the consensus framework across psychology and neuroscience of meaning
**Supporting Evidence**: Multiple longitudinal studies, clinical trials, and cross-cultural studies validate the three-component structure
**Key Distinctions**: The three dimensions are statistically distinguishable but intercorrelated - a person can score high on purpose but low on coherence (directional but confused), or high on significance but low on purpose (mattering without direction)
**Contextual Variation**: Research suggests purpose may matter most for dementia prevention; coherence for trauma recovery; significance for youth mental health

## Extensions and Applications

**DMN Subsystem Mapping** (2024 *Journal of Neuroscience*):
The three-component framework maps onto DMN subsystems:
- MT subsystem (scene/autobiographical memory) = Coherence construction
- Core DMN (internally-oriented) = Purpose generation
- FT subsystem (conceptual knowledge) = Significance frameworks

**Frank Martela's Four Modes of Existence** (2024, *Personality and Social Psychology Review*):
Extends the tripartite model by grounding it in four fundamental modes of human experience - Having, Loving, Doing, and Being - each generating specific needs that feed the three meaning dimensions.
