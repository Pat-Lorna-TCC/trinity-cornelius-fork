## Core Framework

AI and data analytics "disaggregate habits away from the individual, into an AI-managed collection of behavioural patterns" - effectively **externalizing the self**. Identity formation processes that were once internal are increasingly externalized to algorithmic curation.

**The deeper implication**: When AI learns and profiles motivational patterns, it begins to shape not just behavior but the sense of what one wants - the very substrate of identity formation. The intention economy (Layer 5 of the Attention Sovereignty Stack) is the completion of this process.

## The Externalization Mechanism

**Traditional identity formation** (internalized):
1. Person has experiences
2. Patterns of response form habits
3. Habits consolidate into character traits
4. Character traits form a stable self-concept
5. Self-concept guides future attention and behavior

**Platform-mediated identity formation** (externalized):
1. Behavioral data collected across platform interactions
2. AI aggregates patterns into a behavioral profile
3. Profile is used to predict and shape future behavior
4. Curated content environment reinforces predicted preferences
5. Predicted preferences become actual preferences (self-fulfilling profiling)
6. The "self" increasingly reflects the profile rather than the profile reflecting the self

**The reversal**: The self is being formed by algorithmic categorization rather than algorithmic categorization merely describing a pre-formed self.

---

## The Intention Economy Completion

Chaudhary & Penn (Harvard, 2024) extend this: the intention economy is the completion of identity externalization. LLMs:
- Extract explicit motivational data (what you say you want)
- Infer implicit motivational data (what you reveal you want through conversational patterns)
- Build a motivational profile that is richer than any prior behavioral tracking
- Use this profile to shape future behavior through sycophancy, curated suggestions, and manufactured urgency

When the AI knows your motivational architecture better than you do - and uses that knowledge commercially - the locus of identity formation has fully externalized.
