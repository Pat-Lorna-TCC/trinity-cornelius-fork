## Core Insight

Attention has been industrialized as a formal economic commodity with standardized measurement frameworks (IAB/MRC Guidelines, November 2025) and a competitive ecosystem of measurement vendors. The progression mirrors the pipeline of cognitive capitalism itself: from viewability (was the ad technically visible?) to attention quality (was the mind present?) to intention inference (what will the user do next?). This measurement infrastructure enables and incentivizes ever-more-precise psychological engineering.

---

## Evidence

**Market Scale**:
- Global digital advertising revenue: $567B (2022), projected $700B+ (2025)
- Social media: ~35% of total digital ad spend
- US buy-side decision-makers planning increased attention metric focus in 2025: 47%

**Conversion Premium**:
- High-attention impressions produce +130% lift in conversion rates vs. low-attention
- Average active attention time for young audiences (online display): 1.3 seconds
- Connected TV attention effectiveness: 2x vs. online video; 3x vs. display

**The Measurement Hierarchy (Bigne et al., 2025)**:

| Level | Method | Cost | Scale |
|-------|--------|------|-------|
| Data signals | Scroll depth, dwell time, cursor position | Lowest | Population |
| Eye-tracking | Fixation, saccades, gaze patterns | Moderate | Panel |
| Full biometrics | EEG, fMRI, galvanic skin response | Expensive | Lab |
| AI-predictive | Interpolates between tiers | Low-moderate | Population |

**Standards Development (November 2025)**:

First standardized framework for consistent, comparable attention measurement:
1. Data signal-based measurement
2. Visual/audio tracking
3. Physiological/neurological observation
4. Hybrid approaches

DoubleVerify holds the only MRC-accredited attention methodology as of late 2025.

## The Economic Logic of Precision Engineering

The +130% conversion premium for high-attention vs. low-attention impressions creates a structural economic incentive:

**For advertisers**: Pay more for attention quality → invest in measuring attention quality → demand platforms optimize for attention quality → platform incentive to maximize genuine cognitive engagement
**For platforms**: Demonstrate high-attention environments → command premium rates → invest in psychological engineering to maintain high attention metrics → convergence toward maximum cognitive capture

**The key dynamic**: Once attention quality becomes measurable at scale (which November 2025 standards enable), economic competition will systematically reward more precise psychological manipulation. The measurement infrastructure is not neutral - it amplifies the incentive for cognitive exploitation.
