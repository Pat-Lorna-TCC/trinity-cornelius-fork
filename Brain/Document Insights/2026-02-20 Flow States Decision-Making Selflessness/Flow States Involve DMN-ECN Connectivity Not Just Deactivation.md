# Flow States Involve DMN-ECN Connectivity Not Just Deactivation

**Source**: Barnett, K., & Vasiu, F. (2025). Enhanced functional connectivity between the default mode network and executive control network during flow states. *Frontiers in Behavioral Neuroscience*, 19, Article 1690499.
**Document Type**: Systematic Review (9 neuroimaging studies)
**Extracted By**: AI (document-insight-extractor agent)
**Extraction Date**: 2026-02-20

## Core Finding

Flow states are characterized by **enhanced functional connectivity between typically anti-correlated networks** (DMN-ECN), not just DMN suppression. This represents a fundamental update to the "DMN deactivation" model.

---

## Evidence

**Study Design**: Systematic review of 9 neuroimaging studies (fMRI, EEG, NIRS)
**Sample Size**: N ranges from 6-163 participants per study
**Key Results**:
1. **Down-regulation of core DMN regions** (mPFC, PCC) - reduces self-referential thought
2. **Increased activity in lateral prefrontal and parietal areas** - supports attentional control
3. **Critical finding**: Functional connectivity between DMN-ECN (normally anti-correlated)

**Citation**: Barnett & Vasiu (2025), *Frontiers in Behavioral Neuroscience*

---

## Mechanism

**Traditional View**: Flow = DMN turns off
**Updated View**: Flow = DMN-ECN work together

**Network Reconfiguration**:
- Selective DMN suppression (not complete)
- Enhanced ECN engagement
- **Novel**: Simultaneous DMN-ECN connectivity
- Enables spontaneous imagination (DMN) + goal-directed processing (ECN)

**Implications**:
- Creativity requires BOTH networks active
- Selflessness ≠ complete DMN shutdown
- Flow is optimized network configuration, not network elimination

---

## Creativity Effects

**DMN-ECN connectivity associated with**:
- Better divergent thinking
- Simultaneous spontaneity and control
- Creative output quality

**This explains**: Why flow states are creative, not just automatic - they combine spontaneous idea generation with goal-directed execution.

---

## Emotional Regulation

**Additional finding**:
- Reduced amygdala activity during flow
- Reduced DMN self-related processing
- Creates conditions for "decreasing negative emotion while shifting attention away from self-focused rumination"

**Clinical relevance**: Flow states may provide therapeutic pathway for depression/anxiety through network reconfiguration.
