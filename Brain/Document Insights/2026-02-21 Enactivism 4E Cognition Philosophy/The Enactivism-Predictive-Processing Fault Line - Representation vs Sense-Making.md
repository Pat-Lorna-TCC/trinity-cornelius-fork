# The Enactivism-Predictive-Processing Fault Line - Representation vs Sense-Making

**Source**: "Predictive Processing under Enactive: From a Standpoint of Dynamical Systems," *Journal of Psychological Science* (ECNU), 2024; Frank, Gleiser & Thompson, *The Blind Spot: Why Science Cannot Ignore Human Experience*, MIT Press, 2024; "Shaky Groundlessness: A Phenomenological Critique of the Enactive Approach," *Journal of the British Society for Phenomenology*, 2025; "Predictive Processing and Enactivism: Relevance Problem," *Philosophical Psychology*, February 2025

## Core Insight

Predictive processing (PP/FEP) and enactivism share critical commitments - both emphasize the body-environment-brain loop, both reject passive input processing, both use concepts of active inference - but they fundamentally diverge on the **representationalism question**. PP requires internal generative models (representations of world structure) to generate predictions. Radical enactivism denies that basic cognition involves any such representations. Free Energy Principle (Friston) sits awkwardly between these positions, functioning as a mathematical formalization that can be read as either computationalist OR enactivist. This ambiguity makes FEP one of the most contested frameworks in contemporary cognitive science.

---

## Context & Evidence

**The 2024 dynamical systems bridge (Integration Camp)**: A *Journal of Psychological Science* paper maps PP core concepts onto enactivist families via dynamical systems theory (DST). The three core PP concepts correspond to the three enactivist families:
- Prediction Error Minimization (PEM) → Autopoietic Enactivism
- Generative Model → Sensorimotor Enactivism
- Active Inference → Radical Enactivism

Key finding: Within FEP/DST, PEM can happen at any scale where there are **two circular causal loops and dynamical coupling between agent and environment** - this is compatible with enactivism's claim that cognition spans the organism-environment interface.

**The Incompatibility Camp**: Improvisation and surprise-embracing behaviors expose a fundamental PP failure. PP systems minimize surprise (maximize prediction accuracy). Great jazz improvisation embraces surprise - it seeks it. Enactivism explains improvisation through organism-environment attunement without prediction minimization.

**The Synthesis Camp** (Darius Parvizi-Wayne, 2024): Flow states and improvisation CAN be addressed by PP models as "going offline of the self-model" - the self-model stops generating predictions about itself, enabling fluid environmental coupling. This is the PP account of the same phenomenon enactivists explain through direct organism-environment attunement.

**The phenomenological critique** (2025, "Shaky Groundlessness"): Enactivism's attempt to avoid dualism while incorporating phenomenology creates self-inconsistency. The transcendental viewpoint required for phenomenological analysis (the observer perspective on consciousness) is incompatible with the natural-scientific standpoint enactivism occupies. Enactivism cannot fully account for its own theoretical activity.

**Thompson's "The Blind Spot"** (MIT Press, 2024): Co-authored by Evan Thompson (a founding enactivist), this book argues that science cannot ignore human experience - a direct challenge to computationalist PP readings that reduce experience to information processing.

## Assessment of Where Things Stand

The strongest conclusions from 2024-2025 literature:
1. **FEP is mathematically powerful but philosophically ambiguous** - it can be read as computationalist OR enactivist; neither camp has definitively claimed it
2. **Radical enactivism's anti-representationalism is philosophically committed but faces the scaling-up challenge** - explaining language, abstract thought, and higher cognition without representations remains genuinely difficult
3. **Conservative enactivism** (sensorimotor, participatory sense-making) may be more tractable but risks absorption by PP as a "flavor" of predictive embodied cognition rather than an alternative
