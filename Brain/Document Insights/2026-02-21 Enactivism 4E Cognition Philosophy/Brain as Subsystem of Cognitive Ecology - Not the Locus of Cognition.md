## Core Insight

The standard model of cognitive neuroscience treats the brain as the locus of cognition, with body and world as peripheral input-output systems. Enactivism proposes an inversion: cognition happens in the agent-environment system; the brain is one node in a distributed process. The brain does not "contain" cognition any more than a smartphone "contains" a phone call. More provocatively: the brain's structure is shaped by the body's structure, which is shaped by the environmental niche - remove an organism from its environment and you remove the conditions for its cognition, not just its inputs.

## Context & Evidence

**The loan shark metaphor**: The brain is not a self-sufficient cognitive engine. It processes things, but it depends entirely on what it can extract from a broader ecology it cannot itself generate. Like a loan shark, it circulates value extracted from a system it cannot sustain alone.

**What studying brains in isolation misses**: Enactivism implies that single-neuron or brain-network studies are studying a subsystem of a larger cognitive system - like studying the CPU without knowing it is part of a computer, which is part of a networked computing environment. Valid but deeply incomplete.

**The 2024-2025 critical extension**: Liao's *Philosophy Compass* 2026 paper and the *Topoi* 2025 special issue make this methodological critique explicit and concrete. Studying the neurobiology of individuals in **oppressive environments** may be generating systematically misleading data. If the brain is shaped by its cognitive ecology, and that ecology is oppressive, then "normal" neural patterns are not a neutral baseline - they are the expression of an unjust scaffolding arrangement. The "neuroscience of inequality" may be documenting trauma-as-normal.

**The predictive processing parallel**: Even predictive processing (Karl Friston, 2024) uses Markov blankets to formalize agent-environment boundaries - acknowledging that cognition spans the organism-environment interface, not just the brain. The 2024 *Journal of Psychological Science* paper maps PP concepts onto enactivist families via dynamical systems theory, showing that prediction error minimization can happen at any scale where there are circular causal loops between agent and environment.

## Field Acceptance

**Accepted within enactivism and 4E cognitive science**: All 4E approaches agree the brain is not the sole locus of cognition, though they disagree on how far to extend this.

**Strongly contested by mainstream neuroscience**: The brain-centric approach remains dominant in cognitive neuroscience, clinical psychiatry, and most applied fields.

**Partially incorporated by predictive processing**: FEP/Active Inference frameworks acknowledge organism-environment coupling but retain internal generative models as the cognitive core.
