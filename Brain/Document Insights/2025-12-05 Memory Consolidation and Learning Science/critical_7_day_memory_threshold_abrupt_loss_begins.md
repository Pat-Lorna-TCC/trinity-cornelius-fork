# Critical 7-Day Memory Threshold - Abrupt Loss Begins

## Core Insight

Memory retention shows a **critical shift in forgetting pattern after approximately 7 days**. Research tracking memory for up to 12 weeks reveals abrupt memory loss occurring around the 7-day mark - identifying this as a critical threshold for memory maintenance and reactivation.

---

## Evidence

**Study Design**: Memory assessed for word lists and narrative texts up to 12 weeks post-learning
**Key Finding**: Abrupt loss after about 7 days
**Pattern Shift**: Different forgetting patterns emerge after 7-day retention interval
**Implication**: Information at different representational levels shows distinct forgetting patterns

**Memory Phases Framework** (Established by research):

1. **Working Memory**: 0-60 seconds
2. **Early Long-Term Memory**: 60 seconds to 12 hours
3. **Transitional Long-Term Memory**: 12 hours to 7 days ⚠️ **CRITICAL PERIOD**
4. **Long-Lasting Memory**: Beyond 7 days

## Neurological Validation of 90-Day Reset

**Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve**:
- After 1 day: 30% retention (70% lost)
- After 1 week: ~10% retention (90% lost)
- After 1 month: 10-15% retention without reinforcement

**Critical Insight**:
- **7 days** = neurological threshold where abrupt loss begins
- **90 days** = practical timeline when memories decay to near-baseline

The 90-Day Reset heuristic represents the point where memories without reactivation have degraded to the extent that re-encoding from scratch is necessary.

## Mechanism: Transitional Long-Term Memory Window

**12 hours to 7 days = CRITICAL PERIOD**

**Why this period is critical**:
- Memories transitioning from hippocampus to neocortex
- Systems consolidation in progress
- Still vulnerable to interference and decay
- Without reactivation, shift to rapid decay pattern begins

**After 7 days without reactivation**:
- Abrupt loss pattern emerges
- Memory becomes difficult to retrieve
- Reconsolidation required (essentially re-learning)

## Strategic Implications for Content Frequency

**Minimum Viable Frequency**: Contact every 7 days or less to prevent entering abrupt decay zone

**Content Calendar Guidelines**:

**High Priority** (building new relationship):
- Post 3-7x per week
- Ensures multiple touches within 7-day window
- Prevents any 7-day gap

**Maintenance** (established relationship):
- Post every 3-5 days minimum
- One 7-day gap tolerable, not multiple
- Consistent rhythm prevents decay pattern

**Danger Zone**:
- Gaps of 8-14 days = entering decay zone
- Gaps of 15-30 days = significant loss
- Gaps of 90+ days = complete reset
