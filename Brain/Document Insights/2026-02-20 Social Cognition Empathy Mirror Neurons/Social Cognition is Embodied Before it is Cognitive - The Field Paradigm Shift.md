## Core Framework

The 2024-2025 social neuroscience literature reveals a unified paradigm shift: social cognition is EMBODIED before it is cognitive. The body is primary; cognition is secondary. This overturns decades of "mentalizing first" frameworks. Six converging lines of evidence from different research streams all point to the same conclusion, suggesting this is a genuine consilience, not a temporary fashion.

**Field Acceptance**: Emerging consensus - converging evidence from multiple independent research streams

## Six Converging Lines of Evidence

**1. Physiological Arousal, Not Emotional Synchrony, Drives Bonding**
(Chung et al., Royal Society Open Science, 2024)
- Social bonding is fundamentally somatic, not cognitive
- Bodies synchronize in arousal BEFORE emotions converge

**2. Collective Effervescence = Bodily Self-Organization**
(Kronsted, Journal of Social Ontology, 2025)
- CE is constitutively embodied (not just caused by shared cognition)
- Social cohesion emerges from bodily coordination, not shared beliefs

**3. Cerebellum (Motor Brain) Is Part of Social Brain**
(Nature Reviews Neuroscience, October 2024)
- The most "body" of brain regions is now understood as social
- "Social smoothness" requires the same timing mechanisms as motor coordination

**4. TPJ Self-Other Distinction Relies on Bodily Boundary Representation**
(Brain Stimulation meta-analysis, 2025)
- Self-other distinction (the foundation of all social cognition) is grounded in bodily self-representation
- The body-boundary map is the precondition for any cognitive social reasoning

**5. Flow = Inhibition of Cognitive Planning → Present-Moment Body**
(Neuroscience of Consciousness, 2025)
- Peak social experiences (flow, jhana, CE) all involve suppressing cognitive planning
- The body's present-moment precision is what remains when cognition is inhibited

**6. Loneliness = Biological Drive, Not Just Emotional State**
(Tye, MIT; Holt-Lunstad, 2024)
- Social hunger uses the same VTA/SN circuits as physical hunger
- Social need is biological before it is psychological
