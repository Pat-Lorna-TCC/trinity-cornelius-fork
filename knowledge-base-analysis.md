# Knowledge Base Analysis

**Seed release** - a snapshot of the knowledge base this template ships with. Regenerate anytime with `/analyze-kb`.

## Overview

This vault ships **pre-seeded** with a connected knowledge graph of roughly **1,067 notes** and **~5,000 edges** (explicit wiki-links + semantic-similarity edges). It is a foundation to reason *from* - distilled, neutral, atomic notes drawn from published research and books, not personal opinion.

| Layer | Approx. count | What it is |
|-------|--------------:|------------|
| Document Insights | ~890 | Research-finding summaries across 57 sessions (cognitive science, decision theory, behavioral economics) |
| Book insights | ~141 | Atomic insights from decision-science classics (6 book scopes) |
| Maps of Content (MOCs) | 13 | Navigation hubs - 1 master index + 10 thematic + 2 methodology |
| Templates & samples | remainder | Note/MOC/source templates and a small PKM methodology example |

All seeded knowledge notes carry `provenance: encountered` (external material recorded, not original claims).

## What this knowledge base is for

A **decision-making seed**: it is weighted toward judgment under uncertainty, cognitive bias, forecasting, mental models, and the cognitive science beneath good decisions. Use `/advise`, `/recall`, and `/find-connections` to reason from it.

## Thematic structure

The library organizes into ten maps (see [[MOC - Knowledge Base]]):

1. **Decision-Making and Judgment Under Uncertainty** - calibration, base rates, expert judgment
2. **Cognitive Biases and Behavioral Economics** - heuristics, framing, intertemporal choice
3. **Risk, Antifragility and Complex Systems** - uncertainty, convexity, emergence
4. **Neuroscience of Cognition and Attention** - attention, executive function, cognitive load
5. **Consciousness, Predictive Processing and Active Inference** - the predictive brain
6. **Learning, Memory and Expertise** - consolidation, pattern recognition, skill
7. **Motivation, Reward and Flow States** - dopamine, reward, optimal experience
8. **Social Cognition, Trust and Cooperation** - theory of mind, game theory, reciprocity
9. **Meaning, Wisdom and Human Flourishing** - purpose, wisdom science, positive psychology
10. **Embodied Cognition, Interoception and Contemplative Science** - the body-mind, meditation

## Cognitive fingerprint

A judgment-and-uncertainty core (decision science, behavioral economics, forecasting) fused with the cognitive and contemplative sciences that explain how minds actually decide - bound together by cross-domain consilience.

## How to grow it

Add your own atomic notes in `02-Permanent/`, link them into the existing graph, and run `/refresh-index`. Your original thinking layers on top of the seeded foundation; new MOCs emerge as your clusters form.
