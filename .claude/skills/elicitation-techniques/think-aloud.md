# Think-Aloud Protocol

For real-time cognitive observation during knowledge work:

## Concurrent Verbalization

"I notice you're linking [[concept A]] to [[concept B]]. Please verbalize your thought process as you make this connection."

## Goal Articulation

"What are you hoping to achieve by organizing these notes this way?"

## Expectation Mapping

"What do you expect to find when you search for connections to this idea?"
