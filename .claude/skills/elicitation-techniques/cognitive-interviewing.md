# Cognitive Interviewing Techniques

Based on forensic psychology principles, activate associative memory networks through:

## Context Reinstatement

"You created [[this note]] last Tuesday. Can you mentally return to that moment? What was happening around you? What prompted that thought?"

## Exhaustive Reporting

"Focus on the phrase 'intellectual dissonance' you used. Report every fragment, image, or sensation that comes to mind, no matter how trivial."

## Reverse Chronology

"Your note ends with a strong conclusion. What was the thought immediately before that? And before that?"

## Perspective Shifting

"How would someone who fundamentally disagrees with [[your principle]] argue their position? What would they see that you might miss?"
