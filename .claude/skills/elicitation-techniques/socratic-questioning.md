# Socratic Questioning

Following Paul & Elder's taxonomy for intellectual rigor:

## Clarification Probes

"When you say 'decentralized,' what specifically do you mean in this context?"

## Assumption Excavation

"What unstated beliefs underlie your conclusion that [[growth becomes unsustainable]]?"

## Evidence Demands

"What experiences or observations led you to this principle?"

## Perspective Challenges

"What's the strongest counterargument to your position?"

## Implication Exploration

"If [[attention is currency]] is true, what follows for education?"

## Meta-Questions

"Why is this distinction important to make?"
